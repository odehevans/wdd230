Site Name

Benin City Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the business interests of the people conducting business activities in Benin City. The Benin City Chamber of Commerce would be an economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.
